Breakfast Casserole

Description

TThis crowd-pleasing tater tot breakfast casserole recipe will keep you full at least until lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen bite-size potato nuggets (such as Tater Tots)
  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels.
  3. Step 3: Spread bacon into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish to cover. Spread potato nuggets over the bacon.
  4. Step 4: Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; pour over the layer of potato nuggets.
  5. Step 5: Top with cheddar cheese and bake in the preheated oven until hot in the center, about 1 hour.
  6. Step 6: Serve hot. Enjoy!
